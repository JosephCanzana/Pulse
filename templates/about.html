About Pulse

Version v1.0.0

Pulse is a modern Learning Management System (LMS) built to be responsive, intuitive, and effortless for both teenagers and older users. Designed with simplicity and performance in mind, Pulse aims to provide a seamless experience dedicated to the students and teachers of Holy Cross College, Nueva Ecija.
