Pulse LMS — Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to common questions about Pulse — your modern and responsive Learning Management System designed for Holy Cross College Santa Rosa.

General

What is Pulse? ⌄

Pulse is the official Learning Management System of Holy Cross College Santa Rosa, designed to make online learning simple, fast, and engaging.

Who can use Pulse? ⌄

Pulse is made for students, teachers, and administrators of Holy Cross College Santa Rosa, each with tools tailored to their role.

How do I log in to Pulse? ⌄

You can access Pulse through the official school website or LMS link. Log in using your assigned school email or username and password.

For Students

How can I view my classes or subjects? ⌄

Once logged in, your enrolled classes appear automatically on your Dashboard. Click a subject to access lessons and materials.

What should I do if I forget my password? ⌄

Click “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the instructions, or contact your teacher or system administrator for assistance.

For Teachers

How can teachers create or manage classes? ⌄

Teachers can easily create, edit, and manage their sections and classes, including uploading lessons and assignments.

Can I upload learning materials like PDFs, videos, or slides? ⌄

Yes. Pulse supports a wide range of file types — from documents and slides to multimedia — ensuring engaging lessons.

Technical & Support

What devices are supported by Pulse? ⌄

Pulse is fully responsive — it works smoothly on desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

Is my data secure on Pulse? ⌄

Absolutely. Pulse follows strict security standards to protect all user data and ensure privacy.

What should I do if I experience technical issues? ⌄

Reach out to the IT Office or LMS Support Team of Holy Cross College Santa Rosa via the helpdesk or support email.

Future & Improvements

Will new features be added in the future? ⌄

Yes. Pulse is continuously updated based on feedback from students and teachers to enhance learning.

How can I give feedback or suggestions? ⌄

You can share your feedback through the built-in Feedback Form or reach out directly to the LMS admin team.
